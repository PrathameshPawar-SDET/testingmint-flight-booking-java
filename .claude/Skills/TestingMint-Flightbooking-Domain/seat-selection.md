# Seat Selection Domain Knowledge

Knowledge related to seat mapping and selection.

## Seat Types
- **Standard Seats**: Regular seating.
- **Extra Legroom**: Seats at the front or exit rows.
- **Preferred Seats**: Seats closer to the front of the cabin.

## Map Features
- **Aisle/Window/Middle**: Position preferences.
- **Availability**: Real-time status of seats (Available, Occupied, Selected).
- **Pricing**: Some seats may carry an additional cost.
