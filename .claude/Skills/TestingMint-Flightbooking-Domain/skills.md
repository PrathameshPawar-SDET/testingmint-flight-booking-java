This skill provides comprehensive knowledge of the flight booking domain for the TestingMint application.

## High-Level Domain Skills

### 1. Flight Search and Selection
Understand the complexities of flight availability, trip types, and cabin classes.
- [Detailed Search Knowledge](./search.md)

### 2. Booking Lifecycle Management
Manage the end-to-end flow from searching to booking confirmation.
- [Detailed Booking Knowledge](./booking.md)

### 3. Passenger Information Requirements
Knowledge of mandatory traveler data and contact details.
- [Detailed Passenger Knowledge](./passenger-details.md)

### 4. Seat Inventory and Selection
Understand seat maps, pricing, and availability logic.
- [Detailed Seat Selection Knowledge](./seat-selection.md)

## Usage Guidelines
This domain knowledge should be used to:
- Validate test case scenarios against business logic.
- Identify edge cases in flight searching and booking.
- Ensure correct data mapping for passenger and seat selection fields.
