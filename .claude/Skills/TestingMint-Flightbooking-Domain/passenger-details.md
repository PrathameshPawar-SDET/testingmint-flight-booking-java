# Passenger Details Domain Knowledge

Knowledge related to capturing passenger information.

## Required Information
- **Title**: Mr, Mrs, Ms, Miss.
- **First Name & Last Name**: Must match government ID.
- **Gender**: Male, Female, Other.
- **Date of Birth**: Required for age verification (Adult/Child/Infant categories).

## Contact Information
- **Email Address**: For booking confirmation and e-tickets.
- **Phone Number**: For flight updates and notifications.
